GAME MODES

The "easy" button sets the game difficulty to easy.
You will have 45 seconds to eat.
You start with 3 lives

The "normal" button sets the game difficulty to normal.
You will have 30 seconds to eat.
You start with 2 lives.

The "hard" button sets the game difficulty to hard.
You will have 10 seconds to play.
eat start with 1 live.

HOW TO PLAY

- Use the arrow keys to control the snake's movement.

- Choose a difficulty level by clicking the corresponding button.

- The timer starts counting down once you begin playing.

- Eat fruits to increase your score and try to survive as long as possible.

- Watch out for walls and avoid colliding with the snake's body.

Good luck and enjoy the game!



GAME RULES:

- Control the snake using the arrow keys to eat fruits and increase your score.

- Choose the difficulty level (easy, normal, hard) to set the time limit and starting lives.

- Lose a life if the snake hits a wall or collides with itself.

- The game ends if you lose all your lives or the timer reaches zero.

- Eating berries increases your score by 10 points.

- Eating cherries increases your score by 20 points and speeds up the snake temporarily.
